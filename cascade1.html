The ability of CSS to position elements and content on a web-page in almost any way imaginable, is one of its primary assets. Designing web-pages in this fashion lends itself to reusable layouts and uniquely positioning elements. Taking what you have learned about the box model, and adding to it the ability to develop the skills to position content within HTML and CSS, ensures that we are well on our way to becoming web designers. Understanding positioning is a huge step toward mastering web page layouts.
